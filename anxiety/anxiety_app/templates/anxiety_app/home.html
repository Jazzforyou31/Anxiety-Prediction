About the System

Machine Learning Model

This prediction system uses a Gradient Boosting Regressor model trained on various stress and sleep-related factors to predict anxiety scores with high accuracy.

The model analyzes inputs related to:

  • Sleep quality and duration
  • Stress impact levels
  • Activity-stress balance
  • Health risk factors
  • Therapy frequency
